MESSAGE OF PRESIDENT

Smti Mridula Saharia

President, Governing Body, R C Saharia Teachers Training College, Tangla & Author, Ramesh Chandra Saharia Educational Trust.

I extend our heartiest welcome to Ramesh Chandra Saharia Teacher's Training College, Tangla established by Ramesh Chandra Saharia Educational Trust, Tangla in cherished memory of Late Ramesh Chandra Saharia, the renowned educationist and Ex-Home Minister of Assam. We have thrived and have achieved to develop Ramesh Chandra Saharia Teacher's Training College as one of the leading institution in the field of teacher's education of Assam. We provide education and professional development of high quality to the shapers of the future generation.

Our vision is to transform this institute into a centre of excellence for quality teacher's education and training and thereby making contributions to educational and social development. For us, quality education is the most important value and we always cherish this, with the aim to blend knowledge, skills and human values. In this process we shape the personality of trainees, inculcate the right kind of attitude towards life, value of time and hard work, instill to start thinking and respect for human values. These dedicated efforts turn the trainees into a social leader ready for the future generation.
